The project focuses on enabling users to manage video files, apply effects, and export projects in various formats suitable for different purposes.

## Key Features

**Support for multiple video formats including AVI, MP4, and MOV**  
**Basic and advanced video editing functions such as cutting, trimming, and merging**  
**Inclusion of visual effects and transitions for enhanced video presentation**  
**Audio track management with options for adding and editing sound**  
**Timeline-based editing interface for precise control over video sequences**  
**Export options compatible with common video players and platforms**  
**User-friendly interface aimed at simplifying video editing tasks**
